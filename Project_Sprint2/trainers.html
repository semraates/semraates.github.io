Meet Our Trainers

Bob Harper

Bob Harper is a fitness trainer with over 20 years of experience and known for appearing on the “The Biggest Loser”. He is regarded as the bestselling author by New York Times. He is the founder of DailyBurn.com and the creator of the Black Fire exercise program.

Jillian Michaels

Jillian Michaels, apart from being a top fitness icon, is a businesswoman, motivator, mom, actor and health expert. She is famous for her appearance in The Biggest Loser, where her teams won for 10 straight seasons.She is certified by National Exercise & Sports Trainers Association (NESTA), The Aerobics and Fitness Association of America (AFAA), CanFitPro and Kettlebell Concepts.

Tony Horton

Tony Horton has influenced more than 8 million people over the past 25 years with his fitness procedures. His fitness training program includes advanced stretching, post-rehabilitation training, aerobic/cardiovascular training, interval training, resistance training, boxing, circuit training and yoga. He is the designer of the famous P90X fitness program.
